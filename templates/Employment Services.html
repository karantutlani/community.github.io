This program is designed as pre-employment training and focuses on giving its participants the tools they need to succeed. We educate in employment readiness skills, and train everything from demolition, framing, insulation, drywall, flooring, tiling, budgeting, meal planning, and more. Most importantly, participants get real on-the-job construction training in a supportive and supervised environment.
