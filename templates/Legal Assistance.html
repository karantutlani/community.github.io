5 - - Verification - - - - - Referrals to this program result from various sources such as: the Courts, the Crown Attorney, Defence, Duty Counsel, Correctional Staff, Court Workers, community agencies, family, friends and self. The bail verification process involves the completion of an interview to gather information that will be shared with the courts to assist in making a determination regarding release from custody. Following the interview, the bail worker takes the appropriate steps to verify the information provided by the accused. Upon completion of the verification process the Bail Worker advises the Courts, Crown Attorney and Defence/Duty Counsel of the availability of a surety or suitability for supervision under the Bail Verification and Supervision Program. -
